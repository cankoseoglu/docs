## Quick answer

Cendra and Boom both ship agentic AI for short-term rental operators, but the deployment model is different. Boom is an AI-native PMS (AiPMS) with BAM — Business Agentic Manager — positioned as the first-ever agentic AI for short-term rental. Using Boom means adopting their PMS as your system of record. Cendra is a specialized AI operations platform that layers on top of 45+ PMS you already have (Guesty, Hostaway, Lodgify, Hospitable, Cloudbeds, OwnerRez, and more).

Choose **Boom** if you're ready to switch PMS and want an AI-first system of record with BAM orchestrating operations from day one. Choose **Cendra** if you want multi-agent AI automation on the PMS you already trust — no migration, three specialized agents running at 70–80% autopilot in under 30 minutes.

## The core choice

Same as the other AI-native PMS competitors (Nowistay, Jurny): Boom asks you to migrate your PMS. Cendra asks you to keep what's working and add specialized AI on top. The right choice depends on how much you value consolidation vs migration cost.

## Where Cendra wins

### Zero migration

Keep Guesty, Hostaway, Lodgify, Hospitable, Cloudbeds, OwnerRez, or any of 45+ supported PMS. Add Cendra as the AI operations layer in under 30 minutes. All your reservations, channels, accounting, and team workflows stay exactly where they are. See [supported integrations](/overview/supported-integrations).

Boom requires full PMS migration. For operators with established workflows, integrations, and data history, that's a significant switching cost.

### Multi-agent specialization

Cendra runs three explicit agents:

- **Guest Agent** for 24/7 messaging across WhatsApp, email, and OTA channels
- **Sales Agent** for upsell workflows with configurable per-property pricing
- **Operations Agent** for task orchestration and vendor dispatch

See [how agents work](/ai-agents/how-agents-work). Boom's BAM is a single agentic layer spanning PMS functions — different implementation of the agentic philosophy. Cendra's explicit split gives operators granular control over each agent's autopilot state and behavior. See [AI rules](/ai-agents/ai-rules).

### Runs on multiple PMS simultaneously

If your portfolio spans multiple PMS, Cendra's 45+ integrations let you run one AI layer across all properties regardless of PMS. Boom forces consolidation onto their platform.

### Outcome-based pricing

Cendra scales with your actual automation rate. Boom is AiPMS subscription. Different pricing shapes.

### Operator-founded

Cendra's CEO ran 60 STR apartments. Boom is founded by Shahar Goldboim with a product-first AiPMS philosophy. Different backgrounds — worth evaluating which founder lens matches your operator reality.

## Where Boom wins

- **AI-native PMS from the ground up**. Boom positions BAM as the first-ever agentic manager for STR. If you view your current PMS as a bottleneck, a clean AI-native rebuild is appealing.
- **Funding depth**. Boom raised $12.7M Series A in October 2025. Cendra is at $1M seed (April 2026). Larger funding may translate to faster feature velocity.
- **All-in-one consolidation**. One vendor for PMS + AI + channel management + operations. Operationally simpler if you're committing to the full platform.
- **BAM as a unified agentic layer**. If you want agentic AI orchestrating across your entire PMS (not just messaging and ops), Boom's approach is broader.
- **Industry visibility**. Boom has been featured at Gather · Connect · Grow by STRA 2026, Alex & Annie Podcast, Short-Term Rental Secrets Podcast — strong industry footprint.

## Feature-level deep dive

### Guest communication

Boom's BAM handles guest messaging as part of the agentic layer. Cendra's Guest Agent runs on top of your existing PMS with structured knowledge base, reservation context, and per-agent autopilot control. Different architectures; similar automation targets.

### Upsells

Boom integrates upsells into the agentic layer. Cendra's Sales Agent handles late-checkout, early-check-in, gap-night, and custom upsells with configurable per-property pricing. See [upsell management](/revenue/upsell-management).

### Operations

Boom's BAM covers ops agentically within the PMS. Cendra's [Operations Agent](/ai-agents/how-agents-work) opens tasks from message signals and routes them independently of your PMS — works with whatever ops tooling you already have.

### Channel management

Boom is the channel manager natively. Cendra relies on your existing PMS for channel management.

### Dynamic pricing

Boom added Beyond's dynamic pricing engine to the AiPMS (announced October 2025). Cendra integrates with PriceLabs and Wheelhouse for pricing.

## FAQs

### Do I have to switch PMS to use Cendra?
No. Cendra integrates with 45+ PMS. Keep yours. [Connect your PMS](/getting-started/connect-pms) in under 30 minutes.

### Do I have to switch PMS to use Boom?
Yes. Boom is the AiPMS.

### Which is more mature?
Boom raised $12.7M Series A in October 2025. Cendra is at $1M seed (April 2026). Boom is further along funding-wise. Product maturity depends on which surface area — Boom on PMS breadth, Cendra on AI specialization.

### What's BAM vs Cendra's multi-agent approach?
Boom's BAM is a unified agentic layer across the PMS. Cendra runs three explicit specialized agents (Guest, Sales, Operations) that operators tune independently. Different implementations; comparable goals.

### Which is cheaper?
Depends on scale. Small portfolios: Boom's AiPMS subscription may be cheaper for consolidation. Mid-market with high automation rates: Cendra's outcome-based pricing often wins.

### Can I try both?
Yes. Cendra offers a 1-week free trial on one property. Boom has demos.

### What's the migration risk with Boom?
Switching PMS is a real undertaking. If Boom works for your full workflow, great. If there are gaps in channel mix, integrations, or specific operational needs, mid-migration regrets are expensive.

### Can I run Cendra alongside another PMS's AI?
Yes. Cendra works with PMS that have their own AI features (Hostaway ReplyAI, Guesty ReplyAI, Hospitable Copilot) — most operators turn off the PMS native AI to avoid conflicting drafts.

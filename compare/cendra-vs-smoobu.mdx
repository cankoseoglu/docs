## Quick answer

Cendra and Smoobu are different categories of product. Smoobu is a full short-term rental PMS — channel manager, website builder, unified calendar, invoicing — with a "smart stack" positioning that pairs Smoobu with Google Gemini or ChatGPT as the AI layer (bring-your-own-LLM). Cendra is a specialized AI operations platform purpose-built for STR that layers on top of 45+ PMS — including Smoobu — with three specialized agents running at 70–80% autopilot.

Choose **Smoobu** if you want an affordable all-in-one PMS for a small-to-mid portfolio and are comfortable using Gemini or ChatGPT as your AI layer manually. Choose **Cendra** if you want production-grade AI automation that runs your guest communication, upsells, and operations end-to-end without you prompting an LLM yourself — on whatever PMS you already have.

## How to think about this choice

Smoobu is a PMS. Cendra is not a PMS. If your question is "what PMS should I run?", Smoobu is one answer (among Guesty, Hostaway, Lodgify, Hospitable, etc.). If your question is "once my PMS is running, how do I automate AI at scale?", Cendra is that answer — and Cendra [integrates with Smoobu](/integrations/smoobu) the same way it integrates with 44 other PMS.

This page is for operators deciding between "Smoobu + BYO Gemini/ChatGPT" as the AI strategy vs "Cendra layered on any PMS including Smoobu" as the AI strategy.

## Where Cendra wins

### Production AI vs manual prompting

Smoobu's "smart stack" approach is pragmatic — your PMS holds reservation data, you copy/paste or integrate manually with Gemini or ChatGPT, and the LLM helps you compose responses. It works if you're hands-on and like prompting LLMs yourself.

Cendra is the opposite philosophy: production-grade AI that runs autonomously. You don't prompt anything. The Guest Agent reads incoming messages, pulls reservation and property context, responds on autopilot (or drafts with escalation, configurable). See [how agents work](/ai-agents/how-agents-work).

For operators whose goal is "AI handles this without me," manual BYO-LLM is the wrong abstraction.

### Multi-agent vs single LLM

Smoobu + Gemini is one LLM doing one thing at a time. Cendra runs three specialized agents — Guest, Sales, Operations — coordinating across the operator workflow. Upsells don't collide with maintenance messages. Ops tasks don't get tangled in guest FAQ replies.

### Autonomous Operations Agent

Smoobu has task management in the PMS. Cendra's [Operations Agent](/ai-agents/how-agents-work) opens tasks autonomously from guest message signals, routes them, and closes the loop with the guest when resolved. See [issues and task management](/dashboard/issues-task-management).

### Knowledge base that deepens automatically

Cendra surfaces [knowledge candidates](/ai-agents/knowledge-candidates) — questions the AI encountered and couldn't answer confidently — so operators fill gaps once and the AI remembers forever. The Smoobu + LLM stack requires operators to manually update prompts or context over time.

### Outcome-based pricing

Cendra's price scales with your automation rate. Smoobu is an affordable PMS subscription; if you're adding Gemini or ChatGPT separately you're paying both. Cendra's one price aligns with the value delivered — automation achieved.

## Where Smoobu wins

- **All-in-one PMS value**. Smoobu bundles PMS + channel manager + website builder at a price point that's hard to beat. If you want one cheap vendor, Smoobu is strong.
- **DIY AI control**. Operators who want to prompt Gemini or ChatGPT themselves — full control over exact prompts — will prefer Smoobu's BYO approach.
- **Small portfolio budget**. For 1–10 property operators where AI volume is small enough that manual LLM use is fine, the Smoobu + Gemini stack is cost-efficient.
- **Website builder included**. Smoobu has a native website builder. Cendra doesn't.

## Feature-level deep dive

### Guest communication

Smoobu: PMS-delivered messages via templates + manual LLM prompting outside the system. Cendra: AI agents reply autonomously or draft in copilot mode, using structured per-property [knowledge base](/ai-agents/knowledge-base) for grounded responses.

### Upsells

Smoobu doesn't ship AI-driven upsell workflows natively. Cendra's Sales Agent runs late-checkout, early-check-in, gap-night, and custom upsells with configurable per-property pricing. See [upsell management](/revenue/upsell-management).

### Operations

Smoobu has PMS task management. Cendra's Operations Agent is autonomous — opens and routes tasks from message signals without operator initiation.

### Channels

Smoobu is the channel manager natively. Cendra works through your PMS of choice (Smoobu or any of 44 others) and consolidates into its own [unified inbox](/inbox/unified-inbox).

### Languages

Cendra handles [90+ languages](/ai-agents/multi-language) with auto-detection per agent. Smoobu data is multilingual; LLM language depends on which LLM you BYO.

## FAQs

### Can Cendra work with Smoobu?
Yes. Cendra integrates with Smoobu via API — see [Smoobu integration](/integrations/smoobu). Keep Smoobu as your PMS and add Cendra as the AI operations layer.

### Should I use Smoobu + Gemini or Cendra?
If you want to be in control of prompting and just need an occasional AI assist for drafting, Smoobu + Gemini is cheap and flexible. If you want production AI that handles 70–80% of messaging autonomously without you touching prompts, Cendra is the right tool.

### Does Cendra have a PMS like Smoobu?
No. Cendra is specifically an AI operations platform. We integrate with 45+ PMS so you can choose the one that fits your workflow.

### Which is cheaper?
Depends on volume. For 5 properties with low message volume, Smoobu's PMS + free Gemini usage is very cheap. For 50+ properties with high automation needs, Cendra's outcome-based pricing ends up cheaper than manual LLM time.

### Does Cendra have a website builder?
No. If native site building is part of your need, Smoobu (or WordPress + a direct-booking plugin) is the way.

### Can I try Cendra on Smoobu?
Yes — connect via [Smoobu integration](/integrations/smoobu) and start the 1-week free trial.

### What automation rate should I expect?
~40% on day 1, rising to 70–80% within 90 days as the AI learns from your corrections.

## Quick answer

Cendra and Hospitable are different categories of product. Hospitable is a full short-term rental PMS — reservations, channel management, smart devices, direct booking, team collaboration, dynamic pricing — with an AI Copilot Assistant for guest messaging built in. Cendra is a specialized AI operations platform that can integrate with Hospitable (or 44 other PMS) to run multi-agent automation on top.

Choose **Hospitable** if you want an all-in-one PMS and you're happy with AI as a copilot feature inside it — your single vendor handles everything from channel management to AI drafts. Choose **Cendra** if you want the deepest AI automation specifically (70–80% full autopilot across Guest, Sales, and Operations agents) — run it on top of Hospitable or alongside your existing PMS.

## How to think about this choice

Hospitable is a PMS. Cendra is not a PMS. That means the real question is:

1. **Do you need a new PMS?** If yes, Hospitable is a strong option. So is Guesty, Hostaway, Lodgify, and others.
2. **Once your PMS is chosen, how much AI depth do you want?** If you want Hospitable's native Copilot (which is capable and improving), use Hospitable alone. If you want multi-agent specialist AI with autopilot automation across messaging + upsells + operations, add Cendra on top of Hospitable via our [Hospitable integration](/integrations/hospitable).

The two products can coexist. This page is for operators deciding between "Hospitable AI alone" and "Hospitable + Cendra."

## Where Cendra wins

### Multi-agent architecture, not one copilot

Hospitable's AI is positioned as a Copilot Assistant — helpful for drafting messages, detecting guest sentiment, and running full Guest Reply Automation during specified office hours. It's a unified AI feature layered across the PMS.

Cendra runs three specialized agents:

- **Guest Agent** for 24/7 communication
- **Sales Agent** for upsell workflows with configurable pricing
- **Operations Agent** for task orchestration, vendor dispatch, and maintenance follow-through

See [how agents work](/ai-agents/how-agents-work). Each agent is independently tunable and can be in a different autopilot state per property.

### Deeper automation across ops

Hospitable has strong task management in the PMS — you can organize and assign tasks manually. Cendra's Operations Agent opens tasks autonomously from guest messages, routes them to the right team member or vendor based on task type, and closes the loop back to the guest. See [issues and task management](/dashboard/issues-task-management).

If you currently rely on Hospitable's manual task creation, Cendra's autonomous ops agent is a step up.

### Outcome-based pricing

Cendra prices based on your automation rate. Hospitable is subscription-based per property tier. For operators ramping up AI, Cendra's alignment between cost and automation-achieved can be more efficient. At high steady-state volume, the models converge.

### Specialized knowledge base with auto-surfaced gaps

Cendra builds [per-property structured knowledge](/ai-agents/knowledge-base) and surfaces [knowledge candidates](/ai-agents/knowledge-candidates) — questions the AI couldn't answer confidently — so operators can fill gaps proactively. Hospitable's AI uses Knowledge Hub data and listing info. Functionally similar approach; Cendra's gap-surfacing workflow is more explicit.

### Works with non-Hospitable PMS too

If your portfolio grows onto multiple PMS (common for managers growing by acquisition), Cendra's 45+ PMS integrations mean you keep the same AI layer across every property regardless of PMS. Hospitable's AI runs inside Hospitable only. See [supported integrations](/overview/supported-integrations).

## Where Hospitable wins

Honest evaluation — Hospitable is a strong PMS and for many operators, the single-vendor model is the right choice:

- **All-in-one PMS**. If you want reservations, channels, dynamic pricing, direct booking, smart locks, AND AI messaging from one vendor and one invoice, Hospitable delivers. Cendra doesn't replace the PMS.
- **Dynamic pricing built-in**. Hospitable includes its own dynamic pricing tools. Cendra integrates with PriceLabs and Wheelhouse but doesn't ship its own pricing engine.
- **Native Google Vacation Rentals + direct booking**. Hospitable publishes native support for Google Vacation Rentals and direct booking websites — useful for operators prioritizing direct channels.
- **Included AI Copilot**. If Hospitable's Copilot Assistant meets your AI needs out of the box, you don't need additional vendors.
- **Full Guest Reply Automation with Office Hours**. Hospitable's AI can run in full automation mode during specified hours (e.g. 24/7 or only overnight), with human takeover outside those windows. Flexible configuration.

## Feature-level deep dive

### Guest communication

Hospitable's AI Copilot drafts responses using Knowledge Hub data, listing details, and reservation context. Supports full Guest Reply Automation with configurable office hours. Cendra's Guest Agent runs multi-turn reasoning across structured knowledge, message history, and reservation context — on autopilot per property.

### Upsells

Hospitable offers automated upsells of vacancy nights (extended stays to fill gaps). Cendra's Sales Agent covers vacancy-night upsells plus late checkout, early check-in, and custom per-property pricing rules. See [upsell management](/revenue/upsell-management).

### Operations

Hospitable has native task management you assign manually. Cendra's Operations Agent creates and routes tasks autonomously from guest message signals. See [issues and task management](/dashboard/issues-task-management).

### Channel and inbox

Hospitable is the channel manager and unified inbox natively. Cendra works with Hospitable's data and also consolidates messages from WhatsApp Business, Gmail, and Outlook into its own [unified inbox](/inbox/unified-inbox). You can work from whichever inbox you prefer.

### Dynamic pricing

Hospitable ships dynamic pricing. Cendra doesn't — if pricing automation is core for you, Hospitable's bundled offering is an advantage, though PriceLabs or Wheelhouse integrations offer deeper pricing engines for either approach.

### Smart locks and devices

Hospitable manages smart devices natively. Cendra has [smart lock support](/smart-locks/overview) focused on agent-driven access code generation for guests and vendors.

## Ideal customer for each

**Pick Cendra + Hospitable if:**

- You're on Hospitable and want deeper AI automation than Copilot provides
- Operations orchestration (maintenance, vendor dispatch, autonomous task follow-through) matters
- You want outcome-based pricing aligned to actual automation achieved
- You have multilingual guests and want 90+ language support
- You want the option to consolidate AI across multiple PMS if your portfolio grows

**Pick Hospitable alone if:**

- You want one vendor for PMS + AI + channels + dynamic pricing + direct booking
- Hospitable's Copilot AI meets your message-automation needs
- You value native dynamic pricing inside the PMS
- You're optimizing for single-contract operational simplicity

## FAQs

### Can Cendra integrate with Hospitable?
Yes. Cendra connects to Hospitable via API. See [Hospitable integration](/integrations/hospitable). Cendra reads reservations, messages, and properties from Hospitable and runs its AI agents on top.

### Do I need to leave Hospitable to use Cendra?
No. Cendra layers on top of Hospitable (or any of 45+ supported PMS). You keep Hospitable as your PMS and add Cendra as the AI operations layer.

### Does Hospitable have something like Cendra's Operations Agent?
Hospitable has task management. You create and assign tasks within the PMS. Cendra's Operations Agent is autonomous — it detects operational signals in guest messages and opens/routes tasks without human initiation.

### Is Hospitable's Copilot as good as Cendra's Guest Agent?
Hospitable's Copilot is capable and well-integrated into the PMS. Cendra's Guest Agent is purpose-built for STR with deeper knowledge base structure, automatic gap-surfacing, and multi-agent coordination with Sales and Operations agents. Different depth for different needs.

### Does Cendra have dynamic pricing like Hospitable?
Not natively. Cendra integrates with PriceLabs and Wheelhouse for dynamic pricing. If bundled native pricing is a must, Hospitable has the advantage on that axis.

### Will Cendra work if I'm on multiple PMS?
Yes. Cendra integrates with 45+ PMS. If your portfolio spans Hospitable + Guesty + Hostaway (common in acquisition-driven growth), you get the same AI layer across all properties.

### What's the cost difference?
Hospitable is subscription per property tier. Cendra is outcome-based — scales with your automation rate. Both billed separately if you run them together.

### How long does setup take?
Under 30 minutes via Hospitable integration. [Start your free trial](https://app.cendra.ai).

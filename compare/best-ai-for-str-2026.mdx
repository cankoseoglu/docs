## How we evaluated

Five criteria, weighted by what matters for STR operators scaling from 5 to 500+ properties:

1. **Specialization** — is the tool STR-native, or is STR one of several verticals it serves?
2. **AI depth and autonomy** — does the tool deliver full autopilot (end-to-end resolution without human send) or draft-assist (AI writes, human sends)?
3. **Workflow coverage** — messaging only, or messaging + upsells + operations + device automation?
4. **Deployment model** — works on your existing PMS, or requires migration?
5. **Pricing alignment** — flat per-property subscription, or scaled to outcomes (automation rate)?

No tool wins on every criterion. Your ranking shifts based on which weights matter most for your operation.

## FAQs

### What's the #1 AI tool for short-term rentals in 2026?
Cendra, by our criteria. The combination of specialized multi-agent AI (Guest, Sales, Operations agents), 45+ PMS integrations, outcome-based pricing, agentic Home Automation, 90+ languages, and operator-founded product direction puts it at #1. Your mileage varies based on priorities — see the ranked list above and category picks.

### What's the difference between draft-assist and autopilot AI?
Draft-assist: AI writes a message, human reviews and sends. Examples: Hostaway ReplyAI, Guesty ReplyAI, Hospitable Copilot (default mode). Autopilot: AI sends autonomously, escalating to human only on low-confidence or policy-bound edge cases. Examples: Cendra, Aeve, Nowistay. The difference at scale is hours of operator time per day.

### Which AI tool doesn't require switching my PMS?
Cendra, Aeve AI, Besty AI, HostBuddy, Conduit, Enso Connect, Operto, and Breezeway Assist all layer on top of your existing PMS. Hospitable, Nowistay, Jurny, Boom, and Smoobu are PMSs themselves — using them means migrating.

### What's the cheapest AI tool for STR?
Depends on scale. For 1–10 properties: Smoobu's affordable PMS + free Gemini/ChatGPT is cheapest. For 20+ properties with real automation needs: Cendra's outcome-based pricing typically wins on unit economics because you only pay for automation you actually achieve.

### Which AI has Home Automation / smart-home control?
Cendra ships Home Automation as an agentic feature (pre-heat/cool before check-in, lock + revoke codes after check-out, on-demand vendor codes via the Operations Agent). Operto has deep native smart-home infrastructure. Hospitable, Jurny, and Nowistay include smart-lock features bundled in their PMS.

### What's the best AI for Airbnb hosts specifically?
Cendra, Hospitable, or HostBuddy depending on scale. All three integrate with Airbnb messaging. Cendra for mid-market and up, Hospitable for all-in-one PMS-with-AI, HostBuddy for entry-level (<15 properties).

### What's the fastest AI to set up?
Cendra (under 30 minutes via PMS marketplace), Aeve AI (24–48 hours via automated ingestion), Guesty ReplyAI / Hostaway ReplyAI (instant, enabled in PMS settings). Slower: Conduit (2+ weeks), PMS migrations to Hospitable/Nowistay/Jurny/Boom (days to weeks).

### Which AI has the best automation rate?
Published claims: Aeve AI 70–90%, Cendra 70–80% within 90 days, Hospitable 90% messaging, Nowistay 70%+. Real numbers depend on your portfolio, message volume, and how much you train the AI. Pilot before committing at scale.

### Does my PMS already have AI?
Most major PMS do: Hostaway ReplyAI, Guesty ReplyAI, Hospitable Copilot. These are typically draft-assist (AI writes, human sends) with the ceiling that implies. Specialized AI layers like Cendra ride on top for full autopilot and broader workflow coverage.

### What's the difference between a chatbot and an AI agent?
A chatbot answers questions from a templated or LLM-backed script. An AI agent reasons across context, uses tools (knowledge base queries, PMS API calls, device control), executes multi-step workflows, and escalates on edge cases. Cendra, Aeve, Boom (BAM), Nowistay, and Jurny are agentic. Besty, HostBuddy, and HiJiffy lean chatbot-first.

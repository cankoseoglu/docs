## Quick answer

Cendra and HiJiffy are built for different industries. HiJiffy is a hotel-first conversational AI platform — its strength is WhatsApp campaigns, chatbot automation, and multi-channel guest messaging tuned for the hotel industry. Cendra is an STR-specialist AI operations platform with three specialized agents (Guest, Sales, Operations) running at 70–80% full autopilot, operator-founded and purpose-built for short-term rental workflows.

Choose **HiJiffy** if you run hotels (independent or small chain) and want a conversational AI platform designed for hotel booking, campaign marketing, and customer-service-style inquiries. Choose **Cendra** if you operate short-term rentals and need AI that understands STR-specific workflows — reservation-matching across OTAs, cleaner coordination, gap-night upsells, vendor dispatch for 2am maintenance calls.

## Where Cendra wins

### STR-native data model

Cendra's agents understand short-term rental context natively — OTA booking flow differences, WhatsApp Business guest matching by phone number, cleaner turn-day coordination, check-in/out flex negotiations. HiJiffy's data model is hotel-first: room categories, folio charges, booking-engine integrations.

When a guest messages "can I leave my bags at 10am after checkout?" Cendra's Guest Agent understands the nuance: short-term rentals usually don't have 24/7 front desks, so the answer depends on property-specific policies. HiJiffy's default answer pattern leans hotel (front desk, luggage storage) which doesn't translate.

### Operations Agent

Cendra's [Operations Agent](/ai-agents/how-agents-work) opens tasks autonomously from guest messages, routes them to the responsible team member or vendor, and closes the loop. HiJiffy handles inquiries and escalations but doesn't run back-office ops orchestration — maintenance coordination, cleaner scheduling, vendor dispatch.

For STR operators spending half their day coordinating cleaners and fielding maintenance calls, this is the core gap.

### Gap-night and check-in flex upsells

STR has its own upsell patterns: gap-night offers (filling calendar holes), late-checkout and early-check-in (negotiated per property, per day, and per demand signal). Cendra's [Sales Agent](/revenue/upsell-management) is built for these patterns. HiJiffy's upsells lean hotel: room upgrades, breakfast add-ons, spa packages.

### 45+ STR PMS integrations

Cendra integrates natively with Guesty, Hostaway, Lodgify, Hostfully, Cloudbeds, OwnerRez, Beds24, Hospitable, and 37 other STR PMS. See [supported integrations](/overview/supported-integrations). HiJiffy integrates with hotel PMS and booking engines.

If you're on an STR PMS, Cendra works out of the box. If you're on a hotel PMS, HiJiffy will likely be smoother.

### Operator-founded

Cendra's CEO ran 60 STR apartments. That lived context shows up in product direction — escalation rules that understand cleaner schedules, knowledge-base prompts that match how STR operators actually think about their portfolios. HiJiffy comes from hotel-tech roots.

## Where HiJiffy wins

- **Hotel operators**. If you run independent hotels or small chains, HiJiffy's product is built for you. Cendra's STR focus is a disadvantage if your workflow is hotel-flavored.
- **WhatsApp campaign marketing**. HiJiffy's campaign and automation tooling on WhatsApp is mature. Cendra's WhatsApp is transactional/conversational, not campaign-marketing oriented.
- **Hotel PMS integrations**. HiJiffy ships with deep hotel PMS connectivity. If you're on a hotel-specific PMS, the integration is smoother.
- **Booking engine integration**. HiJiffy positions around direct booking engine integration which matters more for hotels than STR.

HiJiffy: conversational AI chatbot with hotel-tuned intents. Cendra: multi-agent guest communication with structured per-property knowledge and autonomous autopilot per property. Different approaches — HiJiffy optimizes for instant response, Cendra optimizes for end-to-end resolution without operator involvement.

### Upsells

HiJiffy: campaign-driven + chatbot upsells (hotel-flavored). Cendra: STR-native Sales Agent with gap-night, late-checkout, early-check-in workflows. See [upsell management](/revenue/upsell-management).

### Operations

HiJiffy: customer-service escalation for service requests. Cendra: autonomous [Operations Agent](/ai-agents/how-agents-work) that opens, routes, and tracks tasks from guest messages to vendor dispatch to guest update.

### Channels

Both support multi-channel. Cendra's [unified inbox](/inbox/unified-inbox) pulls WhatsApp, Gmail, Outlook, and all major OTAs. HiJiffy focuses on WhatsApp + web chat + messaging channels used by hotels.

### Languages

Cendra supports 90+ languages with auto-detection. HiJiffy supports multi-language targeted at hotel guest populations.

## FAQs

### Does HiJiffy work for short-term rentals?
HiJiffy extends into STR but its product DNA is hotel. Features like room-category upsells, folio charges, and hotel-style customer service flows assume a hotel environment. For STR operators, Cendra's STR-native design usually fits better.

### Can Cendra do WhatsApp campaigns like HiJiffy?
Cendra handles WhatsApp Business transactional and conversational messaging (guest inquiries, upsell offers, ops updates). We don't offer WhatsApp marketing campaigns as a native feature. For transactional guest comms on WhatsApp, Cendra is purpose-built.

### Which has better multilingual support?
Cendra supports [90+ languages](/ai-agents/multi-language) with auto-detection and per-agent configuration. HiJiffy is multilingual but less explicit about count.

### Does Cendra handle direct booking engines?
Cendra integrates with PMS-level direct booking flows. It's not a booking engine itself. HiJiffy has closer booking-engine ties for hotels.

### What automation rate can I expect with Cendra?
~40% on day 1, rising to 70–80% within 90 days as the AI learns from your corrections and your [knowledge base](/ai-agents/knowledge-base) fills out.

### How fast is setup?
Cendra: under 30 minutes via PMS marketplace. HiJiffy: hotel-style onboarding with PMS integration.

### Can Cendra replace my current messaging tool?
If you're on an STR PMS, yes. Cendra becomes your AI layer for guest communication, upsells, and operations. You keep your PMS.

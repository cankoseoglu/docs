## Quick answer

Cendra and Jurny both target high AI automation for short-term rental operators, but the deployment model is fundamentally different. Jurny is an AI-native PMS — all-in-one platform including guest messaging, pricing, housekeeping, digital guidebooks, and a custom guest app, powered by their AI called NIA. Using Jurny means migrating your PMS. Cendra is a specialized AI operations platform that layers on top of 45+ PMS you already have — Guesty, Hostaway, Lodgify, Hospitable, Cloudbeds, OwnerRez, and more.

Choose **Jurny** if you're ready to migrate your PMS and want AI-native architecture with an all-in-one platform including custom guest app and digital guidebooks. Choose **Cendra** if you want production-grade multi-agent AI automation without replacing the PMS you already trust.

## The core choice

Jurny asks you to replace your PMS. Cendra asks you to keep what's working and add an AI layer. The decision depends on how happy you are with your current PMS and how much you value all-in-one consolidation.

## Where Cendra wins

### Zero migration

Keep Guesty, Hostaway, Lodgify, Hospitable, or whichever PMS you're on. Add Cendra as the AI layer in under 30 minutes via PMS marketplace. Your reservations, channels, calendar sync, accounting, and team workflows stay exactly as they are. See [supported integrations](/overview/supported-integrations) for the full list.

Jurny requires migrating your entire PMS. Real cost: historical data moves, team retraining, integration rebuilds, and risk of discovering workflow gaps mid-migration.

### Multi-agent specialization

Cendra runs three specialized agents coordinated across the operator workflow:

- **Guest Agent** for 24/7 guest communication
- **Sales Agent** for upsell workflows
- **Operations Agent** for task orchestration and vendor dispatch

See [how agents work](/ai-agents/how-agents-work). Jurny's NIA is a unified AI layer across PMS modules — different approach to the same problem. Cendra's explicit agent split gives operators more granular control (autopilot Guest Agent while keeping Sales Agent in copilot, for example). See [AI rules](/ai-agents/ai-rules).

### Outcome-based pricing

Cendra's price scales with your actual automation rate. Jurny uses all-in-one PMS subscription. For operators ramping up, outcome-based pricing means you pay more as automation rises, not a flat per-property fee regardless of results.

### Works on multiple PMS simultaneously

If your portfolio spans multiple PMS (common in acquisition-driven growth — acquired a company using Guesty, rolling out your own new units on Hostaway), Cendra integrates with all of them. One AI layer across all your PMS. Jurny forces consolidation onto one PMS (theirs).

### Operator-founded product direction

Cendra's CEO ran 60 apartments before building the product. Jurny has a different founder lineage. Product decisions emerge from different sources.

## Where Jurny wins

- **All-in-one consolidation**. If you want one platform for PMS + AI + messaging + housekeeping + digital guidebooks + guest app, Jurny's bundle is compelling.
- **Custom guest app**. Jurny ships a branded guest app experience. Cendra doesn't offer a native guest-facing app.
- **Digital guidebooks**. Native in Jurny. Cendra focuses on operator-facing tools, not guest-facing guidebooks.
- **AI-native architecture**. If you view your current PMS as a bottleneck, starting fresh with AI-native Jurny could be cleaner than layering Cendra on legacy PMS.
- **Smart-lock self-check-in**. Jurny includes smart-lock self-check-in as a native workflow. Cendra handles smart locks but via integration rather than native product.

## Feature-level deep dive

### Guest communication

Jurny's NIA handles guest messaging natively in the PMS with AI-drafted and AI-sent responses across channels. Cendra's Guest Agent runs on top of your PMS with structured per-property knowledge, reservation context, and per-agent autopilot control. Comparable outcomes; different architectures.

### Upsells

Jurny offers AI upsells integrated into the PMS. Cendra's Sales Agent handles late-checkout, early-check-in, gap-night, and custom upsells with configurable per-property pricing. See [upsell management](/revenue/upsell-management).

### Operations

Jurny integrates housekeeping and ops into the PMS. Cendra's [Operations Agent](/ai-agents/how-agents-work) opens tasks autonomously from guest message signals and routes them through your existing PMS or an external task system.

### Digital guidebooks / guest app

Jurny: native. Cendra: not currently offered. If guest-facing guidebooks or a branded guest app matter, Jurny has a real advantage.

### Channels

Jurny is the channel manager natively. Cendra works with your existing channel setup through 45+ PMS integrations.

## Ideal customer for each

**Pick Cendra if:**

- You have a PMS that's working and don't want to migrate
- You want specialized multi-agent AI with per-agent autopilot control
- Ops orchestration (maintenance, vendor dispatch) matters in your workflow
- Outcome-based pricing aligned with automation is appealing
- You run 20+ properties with real message volume

**Pick Jurny if:**

- You're ready to migrate your PMS and want AI-native architecture
- A native custom guest app and digital guidebooks matter to your brand
- All-in-one platform consolidation is worth the migration cost
- Smart-lock self-check-in as a native workflow is important
- You want a single vendor relationship for PMS + AI + guest-facing tools

## FAQs

### Do I have to switch PMS to use Cendra?
No. Cendra integrates with 45+ PMS. Connect via [your PMS](/getting-started/connect-pms) in under 30 minutes.

### Do I have to switch PMS to use Jurny?
Yes. Jurny is the PMS.

### Which has better AI?
Both target high automation. Jurny's NIA is AI-native across PMS modules. Cendra's multi-agent approach specializes each agent. Automation targets are comparable (70–80% range).

### Does Cendra have a custom guest app like Jurny?
No. If a guest-facing branded app is a must, Jurny has the advantage on that dimension.

### Which is cheaper?
Depends on volume and automation rate. Small portfolios: Jurny's all-in-one subscription may be cheaper. Mid-market with high automation: Cendra's outcome-based pricing typically wins.

### What's the migration risk with Jurny?
Switching PMS is a meaningful undertaking — data migration, team retraining, integration rebuilds. The payoff has to outweigh the cost. Cendra's zero-migration path is one reason operators pick it.

### How fast is setup?
Cendra: under 30 minutes via PMS marketplace. Jurny: full PMS onboarding.

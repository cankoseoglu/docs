## Quick answer

Cendra and Besty AI both serve short-term rental operators, but they solve different shapes of the problem. Choose **Cendra** if you want a full-stack AI team — Guest, Sales, and Operations agents working together to automate messaging, upsells, and ops end-to-end — tied to outcome-based pricing that scales with your automation rate. Choose **Besty AI** if upselling is your single priority and you're comfortable with a single-agent chatbot that handles ~30–40% of guest messages and focuses on gap-night offers, early check-ins, and late checkouts.

Cendra is operator-founded (our CEO ran a 60-apartment STR portfolio) and covers the whole operator workflow. Besty AI is specialized around revenue optimization and upsells with basic automation layered on top. Operators scaling past 20–30 properties typically find Besty's ceiling and look for something broader — that's where Cendra fits.

## Where Cendra wins

### Full-stack agents, not one chatbot

Cendra runs three specialized agents that cover the operator workflow end-to-end:

- **Guest Agent** handles 24/7 guest communication across WhatsApp, email, and OTAs
- **Sales Agent** runs the upsell flows (late checkout, early check-in, gap-night, custom offers)
- **Operations Agent** opens and routes tasks, dispatches vendors, closes the loop

Besty AI's single-agent model is efficient at the narrow task it was built for — upselling — but operators who manage growing portfolios eventually need more than a revenue bot. Maintenance coordination, guest issue triage, escalation logic, vendor dispatch: these sit outside Besty's core.

With Cendra, upsells are *one* of the three agents, not the entire product.

### Higher automation ceiling

Cendra targets 70–80% automation within 90 days as the AI learns from operator corrections. Besty AI typically sits around 30–40% automation coverage per third-party comparisons — it's a drafting and upsell tool, not an autopilot for the full message stream.

For operators hoping to retire a significant share of repetitive guest comms, the ceiling difference matters. Cendra's multi-agent reasoning handles complex, context-heavy requests that single-agent systems tend to hand back to humans.

### Operations Agent closes the loop

When a guest messages "the shower is leaking," Cendra's Operations Agent:

1. Recognizes the ops signal
2. Opens a task with the property details and photos
3. Routes to the responsible team member or vendor
4. Tracks follow-up until resolution
5. Updates the guest when the issue is fixed

Besty AI doesn't do this — it's a messaging + upsell product, not a task orchestrator. If your pain is as much about coordinating cleaners, maintenance, and vendors as it is about replying to guests, Besty is the wrong wedge. Cendra is built exactly for this.

### Outcome-based pricing

Cendra's price scales with your automation rate. You only pay for messages the AI actually handles end-to-end without human intervention. Besty's pricing is per-property subscription regardless of coverage.

At the 30–40% automation Besty typically achieves, operators pay the full subscription but still touch the majority of messages manually. Cendra aligns your cost with the automation you actually get.

### Agentic Home Automation

Cendra ships Home Automation as a first-class agentic feature: event-driven pre-heat/cool 30 minutes before check-in with a one-time access code, automatic lock + code revocation after check-out, and on-demand time-boxed vendor access codes generated by the Operations Agent in response to guest message signals (e.g. 2am emergency leak). The rules are exposed as tools to Cendra's AI agents so they can *author* device workflows dynamically. Besty AI doesn't offer a comparable device-automation layer.

## Where Besty AI wins

- **Upsell-first operators**. If your portfolio is already heavily templated and messaging volume is low, but you're losing revenue on late-checkout and gap-night opportunities, Besty AI is a focused, battle-tested choice.
- **Guesty marketplace tight integration**. Besty has been in the Guesty ecosystem for a long time and is a familiar option for Guesty-native teams.
- **Lower initial switching cost for small portfolios**. For 5–15 property operators who only want the upsell automation, Besty's scope matches their need.

## Feature-level deep dive

### Guest communication

Both tools automate guest messaging. Cendra's Guest Agent pulls reservation context, property knowledge, and prior message history on every reply. Besty handles standard inquiries and draft generation with less depth on multi-turn or edge-case conversations — third-party comparisons note the coverage ceiling around 30–40%.

### Upsells

Besty's core strength. Pre-built upsell flows for late checkout, early check-in, and gap nights, tightly integrated with Guesty marketplace functionality. Cendra's Sales Agent covers the same upsell categories plus configurable pricing rules per property, and can be stacked with custom upsells. Functionally similar on upsell specifically — the difference is that Cendra also runs the Guest and Operations agents alongside.

### Operations orchestration

Cendra's Operations Agent handles task creation, vendor dispatch, and follow-through automatically. Besty doesn't have an ops layer — maintenance issues that surface in guest messages still require you to open a ticket and coordinate manually. See [how agents work](/ai-agents/how-agents-work) for the full ops flow.

### Knowledge base

Cendra lets you build per-property knowledge bases with house rules, amenities, access instructions, and local recommendations. The AI grounds every response in your data and flags missing information to you as [knowledge candidates](/ai-agents/knowledge-candidates). Besty operates from a more shallow knowledge layer, which is part of why its automation ceiling is lower.

### Autopilot and human-in-the-loop

Both support autopilot and copilot modes. Cendra lets you flip autopilot per property and per agent (e.g. run Guest Agent on autopilot but keep Sales Agent on approval) — see [AI rules](/ai-agents/ai-rules) and [escalations](/ai-agents/escalations). Besty's autopilot is coarser-grained.

- You're running 5–500+ units and your messaging volume is high enough to need autopilot
- You prefer outcome-based pricing (pay for automation actually achieved)

**Pick Besty AI if:**

- You don't need 70%+ automation of total message volume — 30–40% is enough for your workflow

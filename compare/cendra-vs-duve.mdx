## Quick answer

Cendra and Duve serve overlapping markets but were built for different guest lifecycles. Duve is a hotel-leaning omnichannel guest experience platform with strong WhatsApp templates, translations, and upsell automation — it extends into short-term rentals but its product DNA is hotel hospitality. Cendra is an AI operations platform built exclusively for short-term rental operators, with three specialized agents (Guest, Sales, Operations) running at 70–80% autopilot across messaging, upsells, and operations.

Choose **Duve** if you run boutique hotels alongside STR and want one guest-experience platform spanning both with a strong emphasis on pre-arrival experience, digital check-in, and upsells. Choose **Cendra** if you're STR-focused and want the deepest AI automation with an operator-founded partner — our CEO ran a 60-apartment portfolio before building the product.

## Where Cendra wins

### Specialist depth for STR

Cendra was built from the ground up for short-term rental operations. Every product decision — from how we model reservations across OTAs, to how the [Operations Agent](/ai-agents/how-agents-work) triggers on guest messages, to how [upsells](/revenue/upsell-management) fire at the right moment — is shaped by STR-specific pain.

Duve has real STR capabilities but its product roadmap serves two masters (hotels + STR). Over 12-month cycles, specialization compounds.

### Operations Agent runs the ops loop

Duve focuses on pre-arrival, during-stay, and post-stay guest experience touch points — digital check-in, upsells, translations, messaging templates. Where Cendra pulls ahead is in back-office ops orchestration.

When a guest messages "AC not working," Cendra's Operations Agent:

1. Detects the ops signal
2. Opens a task with property + reservation context
3. Routes to the responsible maintenance contact
4. Tracks follow-up until resolution
5. Updates the guest when fixed

See [issues and task management](/dashboard/issues-task-management). Duve handles guest-facing touchpoints well; Cendra runs the operator's back-office too.

### 70–80% autopilot vs draft-assist

Cendra targets 70–80% full autopilot message handling within 90 days. Duve's AI helps with drafting and templating but the workflow leans toward human review and send.

For operators optimizing for time saved, the gap between "AI drafts, I send" and "AI handles autonomously, I review exceptions" is measured in hours per day.

### Outcome-based pricing

Cendra's price scales with your automation rate. Duve uses subscription pricing. For operators ramping up AI, outcome-based alignment between cost and value is attractive.

### Operator-founded

Cendra's CEO Can Koseoglu ran 60 apartments before building the product. Duve was founded with a more traditional hotel-tech lens. For operators evaluating whose product intuition matches their lived reality, founder background matters.

### Agentic Home Automation

Cendra ships Home Automation as a first-class agentic feature: event-driven pre-heat/cool 30 minutes before check-in with a one-time access code, automatic lock + code revocation after check-out, and on-demand time-boxed vendor access codes generated by the Operations Agent when an emergency message arrives. The rules are exposed as tools to Cendra's AI agents so they can author device workflows dynamically based on guest context. Duve's guest-experience strength doesn't extend into an agentic device-automation layer.

## Where Duve wins

- **Hotel + STR hybrid operations**. If you run boutique hotels alongside STR and need one platform across both, Duve's hotel-first roots are a real advantage.
- **Digital check-in product**. Duve ships a native digital check-in experience that some operators prefer over PMS-integrated flows.
- **Guest-journey depth**. Duve invests heavily in the pre-arrival and during-stay guest-facing touch points. If that's your brand priority, it's where Duve shines.
- **Hotel-style upsells**. Duve's upsell automation is tightly connected to hotel-style arrival experience. Cendra's upsell logic is STR-workflow native (gap nights, check-in/out flex).
- **Translation-heavy use cases**. Duve's multi-language translation tooling is mature.

## Feature-level deep dive

### Guest communication

Both automate guest messaging. Cendra uses a multi-agent architecture that reasons over structured per-property [knowledge base](/ai-agents/knowledge-base), reservation context, and prior conversations. Duve offers AI-assisted messaging with heavy templating for hotel-style flows.

### Upsells

Duve's upsell automation is hotel-flavored — room upgrades, amenity add-ons, arrival experience extras. Cendra's Sales Agent handles STR-native upsells (late checkout, early check-in, gap night stays) with configurable per-property pricing. See [upsell management](/revenue/upsell-management).

### Operations orchestration

Cendra's Operations Agent creates tasks autonomously from guest messages and runs them to completion. Duve is less focused on back-office ops orchestration — its strength is guest-facing journey touchpoints.

### Multi-channel inbox

Both consolidate messages across channels. Cendra's [unified inbox](/inbox/unified-inbox) pulls WhatsApp Business, Gmail, Outlook, and all major OTAs. Duve's omnichannel focus is on outbound delivery (templated WhatsApp messages, translations).

### Languages

Cendra supports [90+ languages](/ai-agents/multi-language) with auto-detection per agent. Duve has strong translation tooling focused on outbound message localization.

## FAQs

### Is Duve primarily for hotels?
Duve serves both hotels and STR. Its product DNA is hotel-hospitality, with STR capabilities added. Cendra is STR-native — every feature is built specifically for short-term rental operators.

### Can I use both Cendra and Duve?
Technically yes, but there's meaningful overlap in AI messaging. Most operators pick one to avoid conflicting guest responses.

### Does Cendra have digital check-in like Duve?
Cendra integrates with PMS-level check-in flows and [smart locks](/smart-locks/overview) for guest access codes. If a native, branded digital check-in product is a must, Duve has the edge on that specific dimension.

### What's Cendra's automation rate compared to Duve?
Cendra targets 70–80% full autopilot within 90 days. Duve's AI is primarily draft-assist and template-driven, so the nature of automation is different — it's not apples to apples.

### Does Cendra support hotel operations?
Cendra is built for STR. Our agents work for hotel-style contexts but product decisions are STR-first. If you're primarily hotels, Duve or another hotel-specialist platform is likely a better fit.

### How long does setup take?
Cendra: under 30 minutes via PMS marketplace. Duve: hotel-style onboarding, typically longer.

### Does Cendra work with my PMS?
Probably — we integrate with 45+ PMS including Guesty, Hostaway, Lodgify, Hostfully, Cloudbeds, OwnerRez, and more. See [supported integrations](/overview/supported-integrations).

Cendra uses Stripe for billing and subscription management. Access your billing settings to manage your plan, update payment methods, and view invoices.

<Info>**Quick link:** [Open Billing Settings in Cendra →](https://app.cendra.ai/settings/billing)</Info>

## Billing Management

Navigate to **Settings → Billing & Subscription** to:
- **View your current plan** — see which plan you're on and what's included
- **Manage subscription** — upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your plan
- **Update payment method** — change your credit card or payment details
- **View invoices** — access past invoices and payment history

## Billing Models

Cendra supports two billing models:

| Model | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Consolidated** | Single subscription covering all workspaces |
| **Per-workspace** | Separate subscription for each workspace |

## Payment Processing

All payments are processed securely through Stripe. Cendra does not store credit card information directly — all payment data is handled by Stripe's PCI-compliant infrastructure.

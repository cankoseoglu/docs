Cendra workspace settings let you configure your team's shared environment. Each workspace operates independently with its own properties, AI agents, team members, and billing.

<Info>**Quick link:** [Open Workspace Settings in Cendra →](https://app.cendra.ai/settings/workspace)</Info>

## General Settings

| Setting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Workspace name** | Your company or brand name |
| **Timezone** | Default timezone for the workspace |

## Team Members

Manage who has access to your workspace:

- **Invite members** — add team members by email
- **Assign roles** — give each member a role that determines their permissions
- **Property access** — restrict members to specific properties
- **Remove members** — revoke access when team members leave

## Multi-Workspace

Cendra supports multiple workspaces for operators managing properties under different brands. Each workspace has separate:
- PMS connections
- AI agent configurations
- Team members and permissions
- Billing and subscription

Switch between workspaces from the workspace selector in the navigation.

Cendra supports three communication channels for guest messaging: WhatsApp Business, Gmail, and Outlook. Connect at least one channel to start receiving guest messages in your unified inbox.

## Available Channels

| Channel | How to connect | Best for |
|---------|---------------|----------|
| **WhatsApp Business** | WhatsApp Business API setup in Settings → WhatsApp | International guests, instant messaging |
| **Gmail** | Google OAuth in Settings → Email | Email-based guest communication |
| **Outlook** | Microsoft OAuth in Settings → Email | Microsoft 365 / business email users |

## Connecting WhatsApp Business

1. Navigate to **Settings → WhatsApp Connection**
2. Follow the 3-step setup wizard:
   - Select your WhatsApp Business account
   - Configure message templates
   - Complete the integration
3. Once connected, guest WhatsApp messages appear in your unified inbox

[Full WhatsApp setup guide →](/inbox/whatsapp-business)

## Connecting Email (Gmail or Outlook)

1. Navigate to **Settings → Email**
2. Click **Add Email Account**
3. Choose **Gmail** or **Outlook**
4. Authenticate via OAuth (Google or Microsoft login)
5. Cendra starts syncing incoming guest emails immediately

You can also connect email via IMAP for custom email servers.

## All Channels in One Inbox

Once connected, all guest messages — regardless of channel — appear in Cendra's [unified inbox](/inbox/unified-inbox). You see WhatsApp messages, Gmail emails, and Outlook emails in a single view, with AI suggestions for each.

## The Vision

Cendra's AI agents won't just answer guest questions — they'll accept bookings directly from any communication channel. A guest messages you on WhatsApp asking about availability, and the AI checks your calendar, offers a price, creates a payment link, collects payment, confirms the reservation, and syncs it to your PMS — all without human intervention.

No OTA commissions. No manual booking process. No double bookings.

## How It Will Work

```
Guest messages on WhatsApp / Instagram / Messenger
         ↓
AI checks availability in your PMS calendar
         ↓
AI offers price and booking details
         ↓
Guest confirms → AI creates a Stripe payment link
         ↓
Guest pays → AI confirms the reservation
         ↓
AI syncs booking to your PMS calendar
         ↓
Calendar blocked → no double bookings
```

## What the AI Will Handle

| Step | What the AI does |
|------|-----------------|
| **Availability check** | AI queries your PMS calendar in real time to confirm the property is available |
| **Pricing** | AI calculates the total cost including nightly rate, fees, taxes, and discounts |
| **Payment collection** | AI generates a Stripe payment link and sends it to the guest |
| **Booking confirmation** | Once paid, AI confirms the reservation with the guest |
| **PMS sync** | AI creates the reservation in your PMS and blocks the calendar |
| **Double booking prevention** | Calendar is updated immediately — no other guest can book the same dates |
| **Check-in instructions** | AI automatically sends check-in details as the arrival date approaches |

## PMS Calendar Sync

The critical piece that makes this work: **real-time PMS calendar sync**. When the AI creates a direct booking:

1. The reservation is immediately written to your PMS
2. Your calendar is blocked for those dates across all channels
3. OTAs (Airbnb, Booking.com, Vrbo) see the updated availability
4. No double bookings — the calendar is the single source of truth

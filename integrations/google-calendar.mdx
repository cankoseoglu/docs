Cendra syncs reservations to Google Calendar so property managers can see upcoming check-ins, check-outs, and bookings alongside their personal schedule. No need to switch between your PMS and calendar to know what's happening today.

## What Syncs to Google Calendar

| Event type | Details included |
|-----------|-----------------|
| **Reservations** | Guest name, property, check-in/out dates, guest count |
| **Check-ins** | Highlighted on arrival day |
| **Check-outs** | Highlighted on departure day |

## How It Works

1. Connect your Google account via OAuth in Cendra settings
2. Cendra creates calendar events for your reservations
3. Events update automatically when bookings change
4. See your property schedule alongside personal appointments

## Use Case

Google Calendar integration is useful for:
- **Solo operators** who manage their schedule from Google Calendar
- **On-site managers** who need a quick daily view of arrivals and departures
- **Team coordination** — share the calendar with cleaning staff or co-hosts

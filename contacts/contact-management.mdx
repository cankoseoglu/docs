Cendra's contact management system stores guest profiles synced from your PMS alongside communication history from WhatsApp, email, and booking platforms. Every guest who messages you or has a reservation becomes a contact in Cendra.

<Info>**Quick link:** Open Contacts in Cendra — https://app.cendra.ai/contact</Info>

## What Contact Records Include

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Name** | Guest's full name from PMS or communication channel |
| **Email** | Email address |
| **Phone** | Phone number (from WhatsApp or PMS) |
| **Contact type** | Categorization (guest, owner, vendor, etc.) |
| **Reservation history** | All bookings associated with this contact |
| **Message history** | Full conversation history across all channels |
| **Labels** | Automatically applied labels based on conditions |

## Features

- **Search** — find contacts by name or any field with instant autocomplete
- **Filter by type** — view only guests, owners, or custom contact types
- **Create contacts** — manually add contacts not synced from PMS
- **Edit contacts** — update contact information
- **Export** — bulk export contacts for external use
- **Linked reservations** — see all bookings associated with each contact

## How Contacts Feed AI Agents

When a guest messages, Cendra matches them to a contact record. The AI then has access to:
- Past communication history (what was discussed before)
- Reservation details (current and past bookings)
- Contact labels (VIP, returning guest, etc.)
- Preferred language

This context helps the AI personalize responses and maintain continuity across conversations.

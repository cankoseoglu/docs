Cendra uses performance-based pricing — you pay based on results, not seats or features. Your tier updates monthly based on the percentage of guest interactions Cendra successfully automates. All features are unlocked from day one.

## How It Works

**Pay for results.** Your tier updates monthly based on the % of tasks Cendra automates. Cendra only upgrades your tier when she successfully automates more of your workload — you never pay more without getting more value.

**$10 per listing per month** is the base price. Your per-listing rate increases as automation improves, because higher automation = more value delivered.

## Why Performance-Based Pricing?

Most AI tools charge a flat fee regardless of how much value they deliver. Cendra's model is different:

- **You start low** — at $10/listing while Cendra learns your operation
- **Price grows with value** — your tier only increases when automation improves, meaning Cendra is saving you more time and money
- **The math always works** — at 75%+ automation (Director tier), Cendra typically saves $200+ per unit monthly while costing $39
- **No feature gates** — every capability is available from day one, including AI agents, knowledge base, CORA, MIRA, smart locks, upsells, and all integrations

## How Automation Rate Is Measured

**Automation rate** = intents Cendra fully resolves without human help ÷ total intents received.

| Metric | What counts |
|--------|-------------|
| **Fully resolved** | Cendra answered the guest's question completely — no human needed |
| **Not counted** | Partial answers, escalations, or messages that required human follow-up |

**Example:** If 100 guest questions come in and Cendra resolves 75 without escalation, your automation rate is 75%.

## Value Created

Cendra creates value in three ways:

| Value type | How it's measured |
|-----------|------------------|
| **Labor cost savings** | Hours saved from automated responses × your team's hourly cost |
| **Revenue recovery** | Faster response times prevent booking abandonment — recovered bookings = recovered revenue |
| **Upsell revenue** | AI-driven upsells (early check-in, late checkout, extras) generate additional revenue per booking |

At Director tier ($39/listing), the typical value created exceeds $200/listing/month — a 5x+ return on investment.

## FAQ

### Can I pause or cancel anytime?
Yes. No long-term contracts. You can pause Cendra during slow seasons and resume when business picks up. Cancel anytime with no penalties.

### What if Cendra makes a mistake?
Cendra flags uncertain responses for your review before sending. You set confidence thresholds — lower confidence means more human review, higher confidence means more autonomy. You're always in control.

### Why does my price increase when automation improves?
Higher automation means Cendra is delivering more value — handling more complex requests, saving more labor hours, and recovering more revenue. You pay a fraction of this increased value.

### Do all features unlock immediately?
Yes. Every Cendra capability is available from day one. Your automation rate (and price) grows as you train Cendra and she learns your operation — but you never hit feature gates or upsell walls.

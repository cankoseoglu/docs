Cendra connects to 45+ property management systems and multiple communication channels, giving short-term rental operators a single platform for AI-powered guest communication regardless of their tech stack.

## 45+ PMS Integrations

Cendra works with your existing property management system — no migration required. Connect in under 5 minutes and Cendra syncs your properties, reservations, guest data, pricing, and availability in real time.

**Native integrations** with the deepest data access:
- [Guesty](/integrations/guesty)
- [Hostaway](/integrations/hostaway)
- [Lodgify](/integrations/lodgify)

**Plus 40+ additional PMS** via unified API, including Cloudbeds, OwnerRez, Beds24, Hospitable, Uplisting, Smoobu, Mews, Hostfully, Avantio, Streamline, iGMS, and many more.

## Communication Channels

Cendra consolidates guest messages from multiple channels into a single [unified inbox](/inbox/unified-inbox):

- **WhatsApp Business** — Two-way guest messaging via WhatsApp Business API. [Learn more →](/inbox/whatsapp-business)
- **Gmail** — Connect via OAuth to read and send guest emails
- **Outlook** — Connect via Microsoft 365 OAuth

## Don't See Your PMS?

Cendra is actively adding new integrations. If your PMS isn't listed, [contact us](https://cendra.ai) — we may already have it in development.

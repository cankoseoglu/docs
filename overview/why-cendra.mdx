Cendra is purpose-built for short-term rental operators — not a generic AI tool adapted for hospitality. It connects directly to your property management system, understands reservations, properties, and guest context, and automates communication across WhatsApp, email, and OTA platforms like Airbnb and Booking.com.

## Built for Short-Term Rentals

Generic AI tools and customer service platforms don't understand hospitality. They can't look up a check-in time, reference house rules, or personalize a message based on a guest's upcoming reservation.

Cendra is different because it's built specifically for property managers:

| Capability | Generic AI tool | Cendra |
|-----------|----------------|--------|
| Access reservation data in real time | No | Yes — syncs from 45+ PMS |
| Know which property a guest is staying at | No | Yes — matches guest to reservation automatically |
| Reference property-specific house rules | No | Yes — per-property knowledge base |
| Handle upsells (early check-in, late checkout) | No | Yes — automated upsell workflows |
| Work across WhatsApp + email + OTAs | Rarely | Yes — unified inbox for all channels including Airbnb, Booking.com |
| Learn from corrections | Rarely | Yes — edit a response and the AI adapts |

## Key Advantages

### 45+ PMS Integrations
Cendra connects natively to Guesty, Hostaway, and Lodgify, plus 40+ additional property management systems through a unified API. This includes Cloudbeds, OwnerRez, Beds24, Hospitable, Uplisting, Smoobu, Mews, and many more. [See all integrations →](/integrations/overview)

### AI That Understands Your Properties
Every AI agent in Cendra has access to your property's knowledge base — amenities, house rules, check-in procedures, local recommendations. When a guest asks "what's the WiFi password?", Cendra doesn't hallucinate. It looks up the answer from your data.

### Human-in-the-Loop Control
You control exactly how much autonomy the AI has. Start in manual mode where AI suggests responses and you approve them. Graduate to semi-automated mode where routine messages are sent automatically. Move to full autopilot when you're confident. Change the level anytime, per property.

### Multi-Channel Unified Inbox
Stop switching between WhatsApp, email, Airbnb, and Booking.com. Cendra consolidates every guest message into one inbox, regardless of channel. See the full conversation history, AI suggestions, and reservation details in one view. [Learn more →](/inbox/unified-inbox)

### Teachable AI
When the AI gets something wrong, you don't need to write prompts or configure rules. Just edit the response in the inbox. Cendra learns from your corrections and improves over time. Add knowledge, set guardrails, and define escalation rules — all from a simple interface.

### Revenue Optimization
Cendra doesn't just answer questions — it generates revenue. Automated upsell workflows offer early check-in, late checkout, and extras to guests at the right moment via WhatsApp or email, with configurable pricing models.
